Executive Summary
SCO · as of May 30

Northwind Holdings is a Toronto-based diversified holding company with controlling interests in infrastructure, renewable energy, and logistics across North America and Europe. Founded and chaired by Robert Johnson, the group runs a disciplined capital-recycling strategy and is an active issuer in public debt and equity markets.

Our relationship: CIBC has banked Northwind Holdings since 1998, anchored by Corporate Banking lending that has expanded into Capital Markets advisory and Global Markets hedging. Private Wealth covers the Johnson family office, Johnson Family Capital.
